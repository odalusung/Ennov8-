Who We Are

Ennov8 Design Group, LLC (Ennov8) is proud to be a locally owned and operated Engineering Consulting company with a strong focus on Transportation Design. We are conveniently located in Central Florida and provide professional services throughout the State. Ennov8 is a Small Sustainability Business Enterprise (SSBE), Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) and Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE). Ennov8 was established based on the following core values:

  • EMBRACE CHALLENGES

    Facing difficulties with a positive mindset, viewing them as opportunities for growth and learning rather than obstacles.

  • INSPIRE INNOVATION

    Fostering an environment where creativity and new ideas are encouraged.

  • EMPOWER TRUST AND INTERGRITY

    Building a foundation of honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior.

  • BE PROACTIVE

    Anticipating and acting on potential opportunities or challenges before they arise.

  • TEAM FOCUS

    Valuing collaboration and recognizing that the best results often come from working together.

  • ELAVATE QUALITY

    Committing to excellence in every aspect of your work.

  • BE VISIONARY

    Having a clear and compelling vision for the future.

  • BUILD GLOBALLY, SERVE LOCALLY

    Providing services and solutions that can have a global impact while remaining deeply connected to and serving the specific needs of local communities.
